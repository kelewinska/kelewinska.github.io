I am a remote sensing scientist interested in leveraging satellite data in service of natural environment and climate change related studies.

My main scientific interests and expertise lie in (but are not limited to) time series analyses of optical satellite data.
To date, I have had privilege to work in several research institutions, universities and private companies in Europe and beyond, being part of projects funded by European Commission, NASA and Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft (DFG). These experiences have allowed me to grow as a researcher honing my technical and interpersonal skills.
